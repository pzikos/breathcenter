IHHT · Intermittent Hypoxia-Hyperoxia

IHHT
Training

Intermittent Hypoxia–Hyperoxia Training (IHHT) is a guided breathing protocol that alternates controlled oxygen levels, aiming to support the body's adaptive physiological responses and energy metabolism.

  • Supports cellular energy processes
  • Associated with improved endurance and recovery
  • Used in structured longevity-focused protocols
02
HALOCAB · Photobiomodulation · Halotherapy

Multisensory
Recovery Cabin

A controlled wellness environment combining red and near-infrared light, dry salt aerosol, gentle aroma and relaxation elements, designed to support recovery, breathing comfort and overall wellbeing.

  • Associated with tissue recovery and skin support
  • Supports relaxation and respiratory wellness
  • Delivered within a premium multisensory environment

Spirometry · Σπιρομέτρηση

Pulmonary Function Testing

Comprehensive spirometry testing measuring lung volumes, airflow rates and ventilatory function. Essential for diagnosing asthma, COPD, and other respiratory conditions in both adults and children.

DLCO · Μέτρηση Διαχυτικής Ικανότητας

Diffusion Capacity Testing

Advanced measurement of the lungs' ability to transfer gas — a critical diagnostic tool for detecting early pulmonary disease, monitoring treatment response, and assessing fitness for therapy programmes.

Paediatric Pulmonology · Παιδοπνευμονολογία

Children's Respiratory Care

Specialist paediatric pulmonology consultations for children with asthma, recurrent respiratory infections, allergies and breathing difficulties. Gentle, child-friendly diagnostics in a calm clinical environment.

Pulmonology Consultation

Full Clinical Consultation

Comprehensive pulmonology consultations covering asthma, COPD, allergies, sleep apnoea, lung cancer screening, smoking cessation and all respiratory conditions — for patients of all ages.
